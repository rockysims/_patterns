Remember to open the console.
  1. Refresh the page.
  2. Notice that reaching the debugger; switches you from Console tab to Sources tab.
  3. Press the step over button to execute just the current line.
  4. Press the play button to continue until the end (or a breakpoint) is reached.

To un/set a breakpoint, click a line number.



Click play to continue execution until next breakpoint reached.

To see the current values of your variables, use Local and Script (under Scope).
